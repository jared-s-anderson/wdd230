Target Audience

This website is targeted towards those who want to know about the local weather. It is also a website that is desinged for locals who are interested in learning about news and local events that are happening nearby.

Personas

Jessica Cruz

Occupation: Teacher

Age & Education: She is 27 years old, and she received a bachelor of science in elementary school education

Reason for using weather website: She will be moving to Idaho to teach elementary school students, and she wants to know what the weather will be like.

Quote: "When you teach one person, you teach many others."

Kyle Rayner

Occupation: Student

Age & Education: He is 18 years old, and has graduated from high school.

Reason for using weather website: He is coming to study in Idaho, and he does not know what the weather will be like or what events will be taking place.

Quote: "Integrity is more important than popularity."

Scenarios

  • What is the weather today?
  • What events are happening nearby?
  • What will the weather be like this week?
  • Will the weather be nice enough for a gathering?
  • What is the latest news in my local area?
