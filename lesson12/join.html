Why you should join us

Rigby's Chamber of Commerce provides many different benefits. We know that everyone has different preferences, so we decided to create four different membership levels. Each of these different levels is the suited to fit different business types.

Membership Levels

  • $100 - Non-profit level: This level is for new businesses and businesses without employees
  • $200 - Bronze level: This level is for businesses with a relatively low number of employees
  • $400 - Silver level: This level is for business that contain around 250 employees or less
  • $1000 - Gold Level: This level is for business that have over 250 employees
