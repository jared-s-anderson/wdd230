Our Purpose

Here at the Rigby Chamber of Commerce, we are focused on supporting local businesses.
Through the Rigby Chamber of Commerce, we are able to provide tools and resources to help the community.
As a team that possessess many unique skills, we have been working together to improve the current state of the local economy.
We know that together we can create a place of unity in the community of Rigby.

Our Goals

  • Promote growth and improvement in the community by helping out local businesses
  • Provide necessary resources to help local businesses grow
  • Educate the community about opportunities to serve and help out the community
  • Inform the community about upcoming events
  • Help those who have recently moved into the community of Rigby
